### Tabs

| Tab | Description |
|---|---|
| **Missing** | Lists movies (Radarr) and episodes (Sonarr) that are monitored but not yet available in your library. |
| **Queued** | Shows items currently being downloaded with a progress bar and estimated time remaining. |

### Sizing

The widget adapts to its container size:

- **Narrow (1×N)** — tab labels collapse to counts only; posters scale down.
- **Wide and short (2×1)** — items arrange in a two-column grid to make use of horizontal space.
- **Standard (2×2 and larger)** — full single-column layout with poster art.
